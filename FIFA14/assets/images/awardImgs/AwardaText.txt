Awards
Man of the Match
Presented by Budweiser. Learn how you can get involved in selecting the Budweiser Man of the Match during every game at the FIFA World Cup in Brazil.

adidas Golden ball
The adidas Golden Ball will go to the most outstanding player at the FIFA World Cup, an honour which has gone to truly exceptional players in past tournaments.

adidas Golden Boot
The adidas Golden Boot award goes to the leading goalscorer at the FIFA World Cup.

adidas Golden Glove
The adidas Golden Glove goes to the tournament's most outstanding goalkeeper. The award was first given in 1994 to Michel Preudhomme.

Hyundai Young Player Award
The Hyundai Young Player award is given to the best young footballer participating in their first FIFA World Cup. The player must be born on or after 1 January 1993.
